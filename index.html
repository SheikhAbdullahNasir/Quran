About the Holy Quran

The Holy Quran is the central religious text of Islam, believed by Muslims to be a revelation from Allah (God). It is organized in 114 chapters (surahs) which consist of verses (ayahs).

This website provides an accessible way to read the Quran with translations in multiple languages and detailed tafseer (commentary) to help understand the deeper meanings of the verses.
